# King Candle

## Definition
A King Candle is a **full-body Marubozu** (open ≈ low, close ≈ high — minimal wicks) on the **weekly or monthly chart**, accompanied by **2× or more the average volume**.

It signals that institutions have discovered or are aggressively accumulating the stock at this price level.

## Criteria
| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Candle type | Full-body bullish Marubozu (wick < 10% of body) |
| Timeframe | Weekly or Monthly (not daily) |
| Volume | ≥ 2× the average volume for that timeframe |
| Context | Ideally at or near breakout of a multi-month base |

## Significance
- Confirms institutional participation — not retail noise
- A King Candle near a Darvas ceiling breakout = highest-quality setup (A+ grade)
- In the Bamboo Method, a King Candle is the "Double Engine ignition" signal

## Real Example — INOXINDIA (Jun 10, 2026)
- Volume: 35.75 lakh shares — **2.6× all-time volume record**
- Candle: Full-body green Marubozu
- Classification: **Type A Institutional Discovery**
- Result: SL revised upward; holding with wider trailing stop
- T1: ₹2,000–2,100 | T2: ₹2,500 | T3: Trail weekly 20-EMA

## Mahesh Rule (Volume Entry Threshold)
> VWMA(50) entry volume must be ≥ 150% of average on the entry day.
> This is the "Mahesh Rule" — applies to ALL entries, not just King Candle sessions.
