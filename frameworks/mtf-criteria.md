# MTF (Margin Trade Funding) Criteria

MTF is 3× leverage using broker-provided margin funding. Higher reward, higher risk. **All five conditions must be met** — no exceptions.

## The Five MTF Conditions

| # | Condition | Detail |
|---|-----------|--------|
| 1 | **Confirmed EOD Darvas close** | Stock must have closed above the Darvas ceiling on EOD basis — never intraday |
| 2 | **Grade A or A+ setup only** | No MTF on B-grade or developing setups |
| 3 | **Profitable, consolidated business** | Positive PAT, no major debt issues, clean balance sheet |
| 4 | **Near-term catalyst within 4–8 weeks** | PLI disbursement, results, order win, ALMM inclusion, etc. |
| 5 | **Stop loss within 8–10% of entry** | If SL is wider, MTF is not appropriate — position size is too large |

## Proactive Signal
> When all five conditions are confirmed → explicitly say **"BUY WITH MTF"** in the output.
