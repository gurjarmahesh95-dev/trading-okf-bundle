# Bamboo Method (Vijay Thakkar)

## Philosophy
Bamboo grows underground for years before shooting up rapidly. The same stocks consolidate for years at a time before a multi-year breakout. The goal is to identify the breakout before the crowd does.

**Core mantra:** "Buy high, sell higher." Don't buy cheap, falling stocks. Buy stocks making new highs from multi-year bases.

## Multi-Year Base Timeframes to Scan
- 3-year resistance | 5-year resistance | 10-year resistance | 13-year resistance
- Timeframe: Weekly and Monthly charts

## Double Engine Signal
The highest quality Bamboo setup requires BOTH engines firing:
1. **Technical engine:** Multi-year base breakout + King Candle + volume surge
2. **Fundamental engine:** Accelerating earnings, management beat, near-term catalyst

When both fire simultaneously → **🔥 Double Engine** = A+ grade.

## Cup and Handle (C&H) Pattern
- Ideal cup depth: 30–50% from peak
- Handle retracement: 10–15% max
- Breakout: Above handle high on volume
- Handle duration: At least 1–2 weeks

## Base Duration Scoring
| Duration | Quality |
|----------|---------|
| < 3 months | Poor |
| 3–6 months | Fair |
| 6–18 months | Good |
| 18 months–3 years | Great |
| 3 years+ | Potentially explosive |

## Portfolio Discipline (Bamboo Rules)
- Max 15–20 stocks. Never add a 21st without exiting one first.
- Master one method — do not jump between systems. Bamboo + Darvas + Lynch IS the system.
- Journaling is mandatory: entry reason, exit reason, emotional state, lesson.
- Treat losses as cost of doing business. A stopped-out trade that followed the rules is a good trade.

## One Strategy Rule
> Do not jump between indicators or systems. The Bamboo Method + Darvas + Lynch IS the system. Refine it, don't replace it.
